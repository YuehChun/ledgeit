# Am I Financially Healthy?

> **As a user**, I want an AI-generated financial health report with actionable insights, so I can understand my spending patterns and take concrete steps to improve.

![Financial Analysis](../../screenshots/financial_analysis.png)

## The Problem

You know you're spending money, but you don't know if your spending habits are healthy. Are you saving enough? Are there suspicious charges? Is your spending trending up or down? Getting a holistic view of your financial health usually requires a financial advisor — or hours with a spreadsheet.

## How LedgeIt Solves It

The **Financial Analysis** view generates a comprehensive AI-powered report:

### Financial Health Score (0-100)

A single number that summarizes your overall financial health. In the screenshot, the score is 25/100 — with a detailed explanation of why (zero recorded income, large bank fee charge, extreme historical volatility).

### Positive Habits & Action Items

Side-by-side view of what you're doing right and what needs attention:
- **Positive**: Low transaction count (not fragmented spending), no recurring subscription bloat
- **Action Items**: Verify suspicious charges within 48 hours, audit income data entry, defer discretionary travel spending

### Warning Flags

Highlighted alerts for critical issues:
- Zero income recorded this month
- Bank fees surged +683% month-over-month
- Savings rate has been 0% or negative for every recorded month

### Category Insights

Deep dive into specific categories with AI-generated analysis:
- **Bank Fees**: "NT$46,398 is an unusually large amount... needs clarification"
- **Travel**: "A single NT$29,900 charge at Taipei Marriott is a high-end discretionary expense"

Each category includes specific, actionable recommendations (highlighted in blue).

### Savings Rate Trend

A line chart tracking your savings rate over time, showing the target (green dashed line) vs actual performance. Helps you see if you're trending toward or away from your goals.
