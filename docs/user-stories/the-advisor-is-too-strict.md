# The Advisor Is Too Strict

> **As a user**, I want to customize my AI financial advisor's personality and budget priorities, so the advice matches my actual lifestyle and risk tolerance.

![AI Advisor Tuning](../../screenshots/ai_advisor_tuning.png)

## The Problem

Generic financial advice doesn't work for everyone. A fresh graduate saving for their first apartment has different priorities than a senior professional planning retirement. Most finance apps give one-size-fits-all suggestions that feel either too conservative or too aggressive for your situation.

## How LedgeIt Solves It

The **AI Advisor** settings let you tune your financial advisor to match your personality:

### Persona Selection

Choose from 4 advisor personas, each with different savings targets:

| Persona | Savings Target | Style |
|---------|---------------|-------|
| Conservative | 30% | Maximize savings, minimize risk |
| Moderate | 20% | Balanced lifestyle and savings |
| Aggressive | 10% | Growth-focused, higher risk tolerance |
| Custom | Your choice | Set your own targets |

### Category Budget Limits

Fine-tune how much of your budget each category should get with intuitive sliders:
- Groceries (15%), Food & Drink (12%), Transport (10%), Shopping (10%)
- Entertainment (8%), Education (8%), Travel (5%), Personal Care (5%)

### Generate Goals

One click generates AI-suggested financial goals tailored to your persona and spending patterns.

### Optimize Advisor

Don't like specific advice? Tell the advisor in natural language:
- "Dining suggestions are too strict"
- "I want to spend more on travel"
- "Focus more on saving for a house"

The AI optimizes its prompt based on your feedback, and you can preview changes before applying.

### Version History

Every advisor configuration is version-tracked. You can see which version is active and revert to any previous configuration if the new one doesn't work for you.
